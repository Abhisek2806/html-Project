1st Planet Mercury

1) Mercury is a planet in the solar system.
2) It is the smallest of the eight planets.
3) It is the nearest planet to the Sun.
4) It is a rocky body similar to Earth.
5) It is made up of about 70% metal and 30% silicate material.

  • The surface area of Mercury is about 74.8 million km.
  • The distance of Mercury from the Sun is 69.67 million kilometers.

02

2nd Planet Venus

1) Venus is a planet in the solar system.
2) Venus is the second planet from the Sun.
3) The diameter of Venus is 12,103.6 km.
4) Venus is classified as a terrestrial planet.
5) The mass and size of Venus is almost equal to that of Earth.

  • Venus is described as the sister of Earth.
  • There are many volcanoes on Venus like Earth.
